The Potential of AI

Over the course of my Spring 2019 semester I wrote a blog about artificial intelligence. I selected this topic, because I feel as though it will become increasingly more relevant in the near future. Additionally, I am quite passionate and interested in the topic. While there are many new developments, there is significant room for social and technical improvement, and I want to be a part of it.
